FAQ


You must enter a credit/ debit card to be charged the booking fee of $25 dollars. For more information please review our user agreement.

Credit Card information is required for our cancellation policy

If you do not see the Course you are wanting to book a Caddy at please contact us and we will make arrangements for you.

Yes, since you are a true sole proprietor you must pay for your own supplies. My Caddy charges you a $75 Start-Up Fee which pays for your Caddy Start up Kit to be mailed to you.

Yes, we do provide Caddy services for Torrey Pines. To book a Caddy for Torrey Pines please visit torreypinesgolfCourse.com or call (858)-452-3226 ext 2

If there is a Caddy that you would like to request you can make that request when you are booking the service on the Torrey Pines website or by calling the Pro Shop and we will take care of the rest.

The job board is where Players and Golf Courses can post a job for any Caddy to apply. Once a Caddy applies for a job request the Player/Golf Course will be able to choose which Caddy they would like to offer the job to.

If you do not receive an offer for a job that you applied for it usually means that a Player/Golf Course has offered the job to someone else and is waiting on confirmation from them to accept the job. Or the Player/Golf Course has not yet chosen who they want to offer the job to.

Yes, a Player/Golf Course can offer a job directly to multiple caddies in order to get their job request filled. Your response time to a job offer is up to you , however if you do not respond to a request the Player/Golf Course can offer it to another Caddy and you can lose out on the job.

This message means that the billing address that you have on file does not match the billing address associated with the card on file.

A user is deemed to be not “active” if they do not respond to another user's request for a job by accepting or declining it more than two times in a row. This will cause the user’s profile to become deleted automatically.
A user is deemed to be not “active” and will be deleted automatically if they try to accept a job without paying the booking fee by messaging the player directly. This will trigger a $100 penalty fee to the user before they are deleted from the platform.
A user can get their profile reactivated if they pay the booking fee’s not paid yet in full with a $25 penalty attached for violation. A second violation will result in the profile being inactive.

For all claims please contact us at contact@mycaddy.org with
- The date in question
- The player or caddy in question
- The golf course in question
- Summary of the issue

Please contact us at contact@mycaddy.org

For all other questions and concerns, feel free to contact us at contact@mycaddy.org
