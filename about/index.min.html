About Us


My Caddy is a contracting platform that allows players to connect directly to caddies to provide services. Players can book services for single players, groups of all sizes, including corporate events and PGA Pro Ams. There are currently many world-renowned golf courses in Southern California and beyond that allow players to bring a caddy to their golf course through our platform.
